What do you mean by Performance Guide?

This is guide to help web developers understand the importance of maintaining good website performance and how you can do that with a few simple things. Website performance refers to how fast your website response is with loading and rendering on the web browser.

This also applies to how your website responds to any type of user interaction. If you have a website that has slow loading times, hard to read and slow at responding to user input you risk the chance of losing out on a lot having visitors on a page.

If you are making a website in hopes of attracting people to it whether it is for a business or portforlio, it is crucial to be able to optimise website performance in a way that not even the user can notice anything about the website performance. User's will always notice a bad website performance as opposed to a good functional website so it is vital that you pay close attention to how your website is running.

Here in this guide we will provide you everything you need to ensure your website is functioning well and delivering your content to users that want to see it!
